Get Involved

Meetings

Our meeting time and place will always be posted in advance on our social media accounts. They are biweekly and run about an hour long.

If you decide to join us, we have a membership fee of $10. We take Venmo, cash, or card. Just catch our Vice President during a meeting!

Social Media

Follow us on social media to keep up to date on our meetings, service events, and other Rotary news!

Facebook Page: Texas Rotaract

Twitter: @TXRotaract

Instagram: @TXRotaract

Newsletter

If you can't make our meetings or have an inquiry, another great way to get in contact with us is to sign up to our newsletter! All you have to do is email us at rotaractatut@gmail.com and say you want to be added to our mailing list. We promise not to spam you with emails.
